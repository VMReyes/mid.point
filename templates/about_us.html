About us


Mid.Point is a webapp created by Victor Reyes, Prado Inciong, and Jasmine Chau. They are a group of up and coming college freshmen who are going to be moving far away from home, to new unknow places. This prompted them to create Mid.Point as a way to stay in touch with friends within their area.

Mid.Point is an application that takes your location and a friend's location generates a meeting point in the middle. Then a list of activies will be pulled up in that area that people can do together. Mid.Point makes planning a breeze as it takes out the hassle of planning a trip!
